# Privacy Policy
This is the Privacy Policy for the upcoming Skypowered App.

Skypowered respects your privacy.

## Data Collection

Skypowered does not collect, transmit, or share any data. All data you enter into the app (such as devices and charging values) is stored locally on your device.

## Data Usage

The app uses your locally stored data exklusively to provide its core functionality, including energy calculations and visualizations.

## Data Sharing

Skypowered does not share data with third parties. No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.

## Data Storage

All data remains on your device and is not accessible to the developer as well as any other third party.
